Scuba Diving

Apart from IT, I am also passionate in scuba diving and plan on being a scuba diving instructor after I graduate. I hope to pursue a career in the tech industry as well as the scuba diving industry as I am passionate about both and wouldn’t only want to choose one and maybe in the future, I am able to find a company that enables me to do the 2 things I love the most. Recently last November, I was in Thailand for 6 weeks to do my Dive Master course, which is essentially a certification below scuba instructor. I learned how a dive centre operates as well as how to conduct scuba diving programs and guide customers underwater. It has allowed me to gain an insight of what the industry is like and I can’t wait for what the future holds for me!
